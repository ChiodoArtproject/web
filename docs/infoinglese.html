Stefano Chiodini

Artisan specialized in glass, a passion that represents the heart of my work. I was born and raised in the province of Milan, but I have been living in Andalusia, in Granada, for 8 years, where I have found love, inspiration, and the masters who have significantly enriched my creative journey.

I founded "Chiodo Art Project", through which I explore the techniques of "Tiffany", stained glass, and "Industrial Art".

Among my achievements, I was a finalist in the "Milano Vetro Under 35" competition, with a piece exhibited at the "Castello Sforzesco" in Milan.

I obtained a diploma in "Industrial Mechanical Technician" and have participated in specialization courses at important institutions:
